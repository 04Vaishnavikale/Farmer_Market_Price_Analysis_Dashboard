## 📌 Project Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis 
of farmer market prices across major Agricultural 
Produce Market Committees (APMCs) in India. 
It covers 10 major crops and 6 cities over a 
period of 2+ years (2024-2026).

## 🎯 Objective
To help farmers, market analysts, and government 
agencies monitor price fluctuations, identify 
seasonal trends, and compare prices across 
different markets and crops.

## 📊 Key Insights
- Mango and Chili are the highest priced crops
- Cabbage and Potato are the most affordable
- Prices peak during seasonal months
- Mumbai and Delhi show highest price volatility
- Overall 12% price inflation observed 2024-2026

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop (Dashboard)
- Microsoft Excel (Dataset)
- GitHub (Version Control)

## 📁 Dataset Details
- Total Records : 4,960 rows
- Time Period   : Jan 2024 - May 2026
- Crops Covered : 10 (Tomato, Potato, Onion, 
  Wheat, Rice, Banana, Mango, Carrot, 
  Cabbage, Chili)
- Markets       : 6 major Indian APMCs
- Columns       : Date, Crop, Market, State,
  Min Price, Max Price, Avg Price, Arrivals

## 🗺️ Markets Covered
- Mumbai APMC      (Maharashtra)
- Delhi Azadpur    (Delhi)
- Chennai Koyambedu(Tamil Nadu)
- Kolkata Koley    (West Bengal)
- Bangalore        (Karnataka)
- Pune Market Yard (Maharashtra)

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards     : Avg, Min, Max Price
- Line Chart    : Price trend 2024-2026
- Bar Chart     : Crop price comparison
- Map Visual    : Market wise distribution
- Slicers       : Filter by Crop/Year/Market
